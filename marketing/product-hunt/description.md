# ContentFlow

**1 API. 21 platforms. 0 clicks.**

ContentFlow turns one topic into a full publishing burst.

## What it does

- Generates channel-ready post variants from one brief
- Routes AI video jobs into publishing flows
- Keeps replies moving with Comment Autopilot

## Why now

Most teams still stitch together scripts, schedulers, and inbox tools. ContentFlow replaces that pile with one operational layer.

## Best fit

- Creators shipping weekly
- Agencies juggling many client channels
- SaaS teams that need integrations without building all 21
